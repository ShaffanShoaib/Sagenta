# ADR-002: JWT Structure and Lifecycle

Status: accepted

## Context
To ensure secure and efficient session management, we need a well-defined JWT structure and lifecycle.

## Decision
JWTs will include standard claims such as `sub`, `exp`, and `iat`. We will implement JWT rotation and refresh tokens to enhance security.

## Consequences
- JWTs will be securely generated and verified.
- Session management will be more robust and secure.
